### Can I have multiple AI agents? <a href="#can-i-have-multiple-ai-agents" id="can-i-have-multiple-ai-agents"></a>

Yes, you can have multiple agents.

You should only have one 1 AI agent per brand, knowledge base or account, agents can also be separated by user group or product where necessary.

### Can you support multiple knowledgebases for separate products in a single AI agent? <a href="#can-you-support-multiple-knowledgebases-for-separate-products-in-a-single-ai-agent" id="can-you-support-multiple-knowledgebases-for-separate-products-in-a-single-ai-agent"></a>

We are able to support multiple knowledgebases for separate products.
